## Decisions Made

1. **Equity split confirmed**
   - Timothy: 35%, Ved: 30%, Aryan: 15%, pool: 20%
   - Ved understood and accepted dilution mechanics

2. **Board stays Timothy-only initially**
   - Timothy explained YC recommendation (CEO-only early stage, expands to 3 with investor)
   - Ved agreed

3. **Founder removal requires majority vote**
   - A founder cannot be removed without majority vote of all founders in Continuous Service
   - Removal does not affect vested equity — vested shares remain with the founder

4. **Revenue compensation trigger**
   - Threshold: $50,000 MRR for 2 consecutive months
   - Allocation: 20% of gross revenue toward founder compensation
   - Originally 3 months, changed to 2 at Timothy's discretion

5. **Post-financing compensation**
   - Determined by the Board at time of closing
   - Capped at 20% of company US revenue
   - Exact numbers TBD when investor is on board

6. **Definition of Cause narrowed to:**
   - Breach of confidentiality (sharing company information externally)
   - Theft or misappropriation of company IP/code
   - Gross negligence in performance of duties
   - Felony indictment
   - Willful failure to comply with company policy

7. **Roles added to agreement**
   - Timothy: hiring and acquisition, product strategy, PRD creation
   - Ved: architectural decisions, technical authority on hires, technical evaluation of candidates
   - Clause added: on technical matters, CEO has final authority on strategy/hiring/finance/product direction; on technical implementation, CTO has final authority

8. **Delaware C-Corp conversion acknowledged**
   - Keep Georgia for now, convert before YC or institutional round

9. **Phase 1 CLI scope confirmed**
   - Detection only (no intervention yet)
   - Three parts: system-level (Ved), environment-level (Dhrit's contact), error drift (Aryan)
   - Tasks to be formally assigned once third person is onboarded
